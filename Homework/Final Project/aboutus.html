About Us

"The perfect blend of bagel & nova meets ropa vieja”


It’s more than just a tagline. It represents the unique business formula that makes Juban’s different from the rest; the combination of a bagel & shmear with a side of daily infused croquetas brings us a wide appeal from affluent foodies to budget conscious college students. Whether you’re a transplanted northerner hankering a bit of Sunday breakfast at grandma’s or a local missing abuela’s cooking, Juban’s has your plate filled.

“It takes one to know one”, Hanukkah and Christmas were more than just toys & holiday cheer in my house, it was about the food – the brisket, the lechon, the potato latkes, the ropa vieja. Mix in a dash of creative marketing tactics and you have a unique well-balanced business plan. All of this is done in an environment designed to inspire and entice the Juban’s clientele.

Located directly across the street from the University of Miami!
