Jorge Valdes

About

Jorge Valdes is a sophomore at the University of Miami majoring in economics. He graduated from Belen Jesuit Preparatory School in Miami. Valdes was a starter on the offensive line and won three district championships with the American football team. Valdes was on the president’s honor roll at Belen Jesuit. He works at his father’s life and health insurance firm. His father, Jorge Valdes Sr., is an insurance agent and certified financial planner that owns his own firm, Financial Designs. His mother, Ada Johansen, is a property manager at JAV Properties.
